Data schema & preview 
sales      (~4,040 rows)  sale_id, date, store_id, product_id, units, unit_price, promo_flag
stores     (30 rows)      store_id, brand, state, open_year
products   (60 rows)      product_id, brand, category, product_name, cost_price
forecasts  (108 rows)     month, brand, actual, forecast
Quirks baked in on purpose: sales.unit_price has NaNs, sales contains exact duplicate rows, some products were never sold, and sales has no brand column — you must merge for it. Unless a problem says otherwise, drop rows with missing unit_price before computing revenue (revenue = units × unit_price).
